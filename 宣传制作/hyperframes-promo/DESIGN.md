# Lingji Jianying Promo Visual System

## Style Prompt
Premium software launch film for a local-first macOS creative workstation. The visual language borrows the restraint of an Apple product reveal: dark studio canvas, large quiet Chinese typography, dimensional product screenshots, precise camera moves, and soft blue system highlights. It should feel calm, capable, and engineered rather than loud or toy-like.

## Colors
- Studio Black: `#08090B`
- Window Dark: `#1C1C1E`
- Panel Dark: `#252527`
- Primary Text: `#F5F7FA`
- Secondary Text: `#AEB4C0`
- System Blue: `#0A84FF`
- Fine Line: `rgba(255,255,255,0.16)`

## Typography
- Display: `SF Pro Display`, `PingFang SC`, `-apple-system`, sans-serif
- UI/Body: `SF Pro Text`, `PingFang SC`, `-apple-system`, sans-serif

## Motion
- Slow premium pushes and scale reveals.
- Use focus-pull crossfades and soft vertical wipes between scenes.
- Product screenshots should move like physical panels in studio light.

## What NOT To Do
- No purple/cyan neon domination.
- No card-heavy SaaS landing page layout.
- No playful stickers, emoji, or decorative blobs.
- No busy grid of features; show the workflow as a composed product system.
